How long does it take to train for a 10K race?

For beginners, a structured training plan of 6-8 weeks is usually enough. More experienced runners may need less time, depending on their fitness level and goals.

What should I eat before a 10K race?

Eat a light meal rich in carbohydrates and low in fat and fiber about 2-3 hours before the race. Good options include oatmeal, a banana with peanut butter, or toast with honey.

How can I prevent injuries while training for a 10K?

Incorporate a proper warm-up, cross-training, strength exercises, and rest days into your plan. Gradually increase mileage and listen to your body to avoid overuse injuries.
